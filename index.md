## Biography

Dr. Amanda Stent is the NLP architect and team lead for the People and Language AI team in the Data Science Group of the Chief Technology Office at [Bloomberg](https://www.techatbloomberg.com/). Previously she has held positions as Director, Research and Principal Research Scientist at Yahoo, as Principal Member of Technical Staff at AT&T Labs â€” Research, and as associate professor in the Computer Science Department at Stony Brook University in Stony Brook, NY. She holds a Ph.D. in computer science from the University of Rochester. She has authored over 100 papers on natural language processing and is co-inventor on over 30 patents. She is one of the inaugural editors-in-chief of [ACL Rolling Review](https://aclrollingreview.org).
